Lady Pixelington, the most sought-after bachelorette in all of Habbo, has been exchanging secret love letters with one of ten suitors.

Rumours have been circulating that the one that has stolen her heart is a renowned maze builder. During the construction of [Collab] Valentines, her letters have been scattered, leaving her unsure which admirer is truly worthy of her heart.

As you navigate [Collab] Valentines, you must recover six of her lost letters and piece together the clues. Each letter (represented by a trophy) reveals clues, slowly eliminating suitors who don’t match her heart’s desire.

At the end of the journey, you must reveal to Lady Pixelington the suitor you believe has truly won her heart. Choose correctly, and love will triumph and you’ll earn 3 Love Tickets

Below are the ten suitors that Lady Pixelington may have been writing to. More information about each Habbo can be found by clicking Clicking will eliminate them, you can click the suitor card again to undo this.

Once only one Habbo remains, you will be able to submit the suitor, you believe, Lady Pixelington was writing to.
